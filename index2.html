Aavishkar 2025 - The Annual College Fest

Date: October 15th - October 18th, 2025

Technical & Non-Technical Events

Aavishkar 2025 brings a series of exciting events for the tech enthusiasts and creative minds. Participate and win big!

  • Robo-War: A battle of the best-engineered robots.
  • Code-a-thon: 24-hour coding challenge for the sharpest programmers.
  • Hackathon: Solve real-world problems in a limited time.
  • Poster Presentation: Showcase your research and innovative ideas.
  • Gaming Arena: eSports tournaments for popular games.
  • Debate Competition: A war of words on current topics.
  • Business Plan Competition: Pitch your startup ideas to a panel of experts.
  • Treasure Hunt: A campus-wide quest to find hidden treasures.

Cultural Activities

Celebrate art, music, and performance with a wide range of cultural events. Let your talent shine!

  • Battle of Bands: Rock the stage with your musical talent.
  • Solo & Group Dance: From classical to contemporary, show your best moves.
  • Fashion Show: Walk the ramp and showcase your style.
  • Drama & Skit: A platform for aspiring actors to perform.
  • Stand-up Comedy: Make the crowd laugh with your wit.
  • Musical Night: Featuring a special performance by a celebrity artist.
  • Photography Exhibition: Display your best shots.
  • Art & Craft Exhibition: Showcase your creativity through various mediums.
